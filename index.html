    • Welcome!

    This is a book about color, and more truthfully, this is a book about understanding the world around you.

    Arbor's Guide (this website!) and Arbor's Guide to Color (a yet to be released book compiling much of this site) were officially started in January of 2025. Arbor's Guide is intended to be a centralized, practical, and comprehensive source of information and tools to help myself and others understand the world around them, with a special focus on all topics related to color.

    Perhaps the most important and emergently complex questions that we as people can ever pose, begin simply, with ‘why.’

    An extensive discussion of the origins and objectives of Arbor's Guide can be found on this page, and information on all other contributor's to the project can be found on this page. With the introduction out of the way, go off and explore this Guide!
